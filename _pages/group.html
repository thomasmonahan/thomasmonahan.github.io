Our group works at the intersection of ocean and coastal physics, satellite and in-situ Earth observation, and probabilistic machine learning. We develop methods that are scientifically informative, operationally useful, and directly applicable as geophysical corrections for Earth-observation missions. We constantly strive to push the bounds of what is possible with data-driven methods, using our physical understanding to exploit different observation modalities in creative new ways.
